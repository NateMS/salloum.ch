My full day in Vienna I spent mostly at the palace Schönbrunn. It‘s a big palace with an enormous garden turned into a museum, located a bit away from the centre of the city. After I did the museum tour, I spent a lot of time in the gardens, by the end I still had only seen a small part of it.
