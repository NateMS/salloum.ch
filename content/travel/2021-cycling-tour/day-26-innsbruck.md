My last day of rest before I will be back home again. I used my day of mainly to relax and just spent my time walking around the old town, sitting in caf√©s and going window shopping. I would have loved to go up the mountain, as there is a way to go up directly from the city, but as it was foggy I would not have had a good view from up there. I enjoyed this little break to get some energy and planned the last part of the route. I would definitely love to come back to Innsbruck some day to see the surrounding mountains and go for a hike.<figure class="wp-block-image size-large">
