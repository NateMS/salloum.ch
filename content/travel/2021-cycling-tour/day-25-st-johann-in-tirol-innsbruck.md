I really appreciated the tiny little hut, as it meant I could get dressed for the rainy ride with a roof over my head. It was a day full of rain, wich also meant I didn‘t take any pictures. It‘s unfortunate, as there should be a nice view of the mountains surrounding me. I cycled straight towards Innsbruck without many breaks. Once in Innsbruck, the rain had stopped and I walked around the city for a bit, occasionally even seeing a mountain in the background of the beautiful buildings in the old town.<figure class="wp-block-image size-large">
